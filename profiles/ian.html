Biography

Ian is from Edmonton and most recently completed undergraduate degrees in Computer Science and Applied Statistics at MacEwan University. He has always held a deep interest in science, and decided to pursue a Master's Degree in Chemistry at the University of Alberta. His current research is focused on applying machine learning techniques in the discovery of novel organic optical materials. In his spare time he enjoys listening to music, volunteering in his community, playing board games, reading science fiction, and exploring Edmonton.
