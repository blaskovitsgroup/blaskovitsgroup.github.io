Welcome! We are an interdisciplinary research group at the interface of artificial intelligence, quantum chemistry and computational materials. We are a diverse and international team of chemists, physicists, computing scientists and mathematicians located at the University of Alberta Department of Chemistry and the Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute. Our aim is to discover molecules for energy, optics and catalysis - addressing global challenges at molecular scale.
